About Us

Where Innovation Meets Global Excellence

Pak-Austria Fachhochschule: Institute of Applied Sciences and Technology (PAF-IAST) is a public science and technology university established under a landmark collaboration between the Government of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Austrian and Chinese universities.

Inaugurated by former Prime Minister Imran Khan on 17 September 2020, PAF-IAST is Pakistan's first foreign-model engineering institute — built on a purpose-designed campus featuring a natural lake, smart classrooms, advanced labs, and a high-performance computing centre.

The institute focuses on Artificial Intelligence, Railway Engineering, Nanotechnology, Mineral Resources and Agricultural Technologies — preparing students for a rapidly evolving global job market.
